Privacy Policy

JDM Underground Content Scheduler  ·  Effective Date: March 9, 2026

1. Overview

JDM Underground ("we," "us," or "our") operates the JDM Underground Content Scheduler (the "App"), a self-hosted tool used internally by JDM Underground to schedule and publish content to our own Pinterest business account via the Pinterest API v5. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the App.

2. Information We Access

When the App connects to the Pinterest API, it accesses the following on behalf of the authorized JDM Underground Pinterest business account:

  • Pinterest account profile information (username, account ID)
  • Board and board section names and IDs
  • Pin metadata (title, description, link, image, board assignment)
  • Basic pin analytics (impressions, clicks, saves) for performance review

This App is used exclusively for the JDM Underground business account. It does not collect, store, or process data from any third-party Pinterest users.

3. How We Use This Information

Information accessed through the Pinterest API is used solely to:

  • Schedule and publish pins to JDM Underground Pinterest boards and board sections
  • Retrieve board and section IDs to correctly route scheduled content
  • Review pin performance analytics to improve content strategy

We do not use this information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the JDM Underground Pinterest content pipeline.

4. Data Storage

The App is self-hosted on a local device owned and operated by JDM Underground. No Pinterest data is transmitted to external third-party servers. OAuth access tokens are stored locally in encrypted form and are used exclusively to authenticate API requests on behalf of our own account.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any data accessed through the Pinterest API with any third party. All data remains within our internal systems.

6. Third-Party Services

This App interfaces with the Pinterest API, which is governed by Pinterest's own Privacy Policy and Developer Policies. We comply fully with Pinterest's API terms of service.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of API credentials and any data accessed through the Pinterest API, including local storage of tokens with restricted access permissions and regular token rotation.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is an internal business tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
