JDM Underground
Content Scheduler

A self-hosted Pinterest scheduling tool built for the JDM Underground business account — automating consistent content delivery to boards and board sections via the Pinterest API v5.

What It Does

📌

Automated Pin Scheduling

Queues and publishes pins to our Pinterest business account on a consistent daily schedule without manual effort.

📂

Board Section Support

Posts pins directly to specific board sections — a feature missing from most third-party schedulers.

📊

Performance Analytics

Tracks pin impressions, clicks, and saves to identify top-performing content by make, model, and style.

🖥️

Self-Hosted

Runs locally on our own hardware. No third-party servers. No subscription fees. Full control over our data.

About JDM Underground

JDM Underground is an established JDM automotive apparel and merchandise brand with an active Pinterest business account and Etsy storefront. This scheduling tool was developed internally to support our content marketing operations.
